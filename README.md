 Sales Performance Dashboard 📊

 Overview
A 3-page interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 
e-commerce sales data to identify revenue trends, 
traffic sources, and revenue leakage patterns.

 Business Questions Answered
- Which product drives the most revenue?
- Which traffic source brings most customers?
- Where is revenue leakage happening?
- What are the refund trends over time?

 Key Insights
- Mr. Fuzzy generates 60% of total revenue (₹1.21M)
  but accounts for 72.5% of all refunds
- Google Search drives 81% of total traffic
- Total revenue leakage = ₹85,338
- October shows highest refund spike

 Dashboard Pages
- **Page 1:** Sales Performance Summary
- **Page 2:** Traffic and Funnel Analysis
- **Page 3:** Revenue Leakage and Refund Analysis

 Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX
- Data Modeling
